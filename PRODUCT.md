# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Job seekers managing resumes, opportunities, applications, outreach, and interview preparation across desktop and mobile. Public CV visitors include recruiters and hiring managers opening shared links, often from messaging apps on a phone.

## Product Purpose

JobPilot AI is a review-first career workspace that helps candidates turn a verified CV into stronger job matches, controlled applications, professional outreach, and a shareable public profile. Success means users can confidently complete core career workflows without losing control of their data or what gets sent.

## Brand Personality

Professional, calm, and trustworthy. The product should feel capable and considered without becoming corporate, flashy, or intimidating.

## Anti-references

Avoid demo-like AI dashboards, oversized marketing treatments inside task screens, decorative glass effects, cramped desktop layouts scaled down for mobile, and social-network mimicry that does not provide useful interaction.

## Design Principles

- Keep the user in control: every external action remains review-first and explicit.
- Make career information easy to scan, compare, and act on.
- Adapt workflows structurally for touch and narrow screens instead of shrinking desktop UI.
- Present public career profiles with recruiter-friendly clarity and credible restraint.
- Keep private data private by default and make publishing choices understandable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, keyboard access, focus visibility, semantic structure, and touch targets. Support reduced motion and layouts down to 320 CSS pixels without horizontal page overflow or clipped content.
